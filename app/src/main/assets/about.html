Snake is a classic arcade game that has been popular for decades. In this game, the player controls a snake that moves around the screen, eating food to grow longer. As the snake gets longer, it becomes harder to control and the game becomes more challenging. The goal is to eat as much food as possible without crashing into the walls or the snake's own tail, which results in game over. As the game progresses, the snake moves faster and the food becomes harder to catch, making it even more challenging.
